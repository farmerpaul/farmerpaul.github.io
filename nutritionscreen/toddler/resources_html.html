General Healthy Eating 

Make the most of every day with great tasting, good-for-you food for the family.

Includes tips on the food groups and healthy eating www.healthycanadians.gc.ca/eating-nutrition/healthy-eating-saine-alimentation/index-eng.php

If your child is 24 months or older, Canada’s Food Guide can be used as a great tool for choosing foods to stay healthy. Follow Canada’s Food Guide to get the nutrients and energy your child needs daily for optimum growth and development www.hc-sc.gc.ca/fn-an/food-guide-aliment/index-eng.php
