Healthy Toddler Survey: Your Results

You have completed the Healthy Toddler survey. You can now look at your Nutri-eSTEP Results. Click on the brown bar on the left-hand side to see What Is Going Well; OR click on the green bar on the right-hand side to see What To Work On.

You will be able to read the Feedback for each question in the section you choose.
Click on Learn More to read more details on your Feedback.
At any time, you can also choose to move to the other section by clicking on the green or brown bar.

Once you have finished reviewing Your Results, you can use the buttons at the bottom to Print or Email yourself a copy of Your Results OR go on to the Resources section to find helpful links to articles, videos, interactive tools and recipes.
