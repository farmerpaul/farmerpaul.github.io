Eating Habits Survey: Your Resources

Resources and tools to help with daily food choices:

General Healthy Eating
Make the most of every day with great tasting, good-for-you food that helps you to live life well.

Meal Planning
Plan ahead so you’ll have food on hand to make delicious meals. Find great tips to get started or improve what you already do.

Shopping
Healthy eating starts with healthy choices made at the grocery store. If you need to make changes to the food you eat, these articles and videos can help.

Everyday Cooking
Find creative ideas that help to make cooking easier, healthier and more enjoyable.

Stay Well Hydrated
As we age, we may not feel thirsty even if our body needs more fluids. Review these articles for healthy ways to get enough fluids.

Managing Chewing Problems
Find every day ways to take care of your gums and teeth or dentures to avoid or manage chewing problems. It’s one way to help you live independently longer.

Keep a Healthy Weight
With a healthy body weight, you’ll reduce your risks for health problems and feel great. Find safe and healthy ways to do just that.

Eating Less Sodium
Cut back on sodium, not flavour and find out how that can improve your health.

Eating Out
Enjoy good-for-you food while eating out with these easy ideas.

Digestive Health
Help your overall good health by taking care of your digestive system.

Prevent or Manage Health Conditions
For a healthier and happier you, find ways to reduce your risk for or take control of: diabetes, heart disease and osteoporosis.

Food Safety
Keep your food tasty and safe to eat.

Active Living/Physical Activity
It’s never too late to enjoy the benefits of being more active. Find out how to get started or discover more ways you can enjoy being active.
